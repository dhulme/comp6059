Easy Design: User Manual

David Hulme - dh7g10@soton.ac.uk

This user manual will explain and illustrate the functionality of Easy Design. For each section written instructions and an accompanying video are included.

Note: HTML5 H.264/WebM video is used, and may not be supported on older browsers.

Contents

Discover Templates

There are several ways to discover templates on Easy Design. Each method is explained below.

Browsing by Category

The quickest way to discover new content is to browse by category.

  1. Visit the discover page by clicking on Discover in the top menu bar, to navigate to /discover.
  2. Choose a category on the left to view all templates from that category.
  3. At the top of the page you will be shown a list of the three most highly rated, popular and newest templates in that category. Below this, a list of all templates in the category will be shown.

Searching

You can search for a template by keywords from the title or description. This can be done either from the home page or the discover page. Simply enter a search term (or terms) into the search box and press Enter.

Recommendations

If you're signed in to Easy Design, templates will be recommended to you based on the templates you have previously downloaded. You'll find your recommendations on the home page. To get to the home page, click on Easy Design in the top menu bar.

Download Templates

Easy Design allows registered members to download high quality versions of the templates for you to edit and use for whatever you wish.

  1. Sign in to your Easy Design account by clicking Sign in in the top menu bar. If you don't have an account you can create one through the sign in page.
  2. Find a template you wish to download through the Discover page.
  3. Click Download to download a high quality image of the template.

Review Templates

Once you have downloaded a template you are able to rate and review it.

  1. Ensure you are signed in to your Easy Design account.
  2. Find a template you wish to review through the Discover page.
  3. Click Add Review.
  4. Click on the number of stars you want to give the template, and enter a review if you wish.
  5. Click Create Review.

Upload Templates

There are several steps to upload templates to Easy Design. Each one is explained below.

  1. Ensure you are signed in to your Easy Design account.
  2. Click on Upload in the top menu bar, or navigate to /upload.
  3. Enter a name and description for your template. Also choose which category your template best matches.
  4. Choose the image you want to upload. When you're done click Upload.

System Management

If you are an administrator of Easy Design you are able to perform a variety of administrative tasks.

Edit or Delete Templates

You are able to edit or delete templates submitted by any user.

  1. Ensure you are signed in to your Easy Design administrator account. Check that ADMIN is displayed in the top menu bar.
  2. Find a template you wish to manage through the Discover page.
  3. Click Delete Template to delete the template, or Edit Template to edit the template.
  4. If you are editing the template, you are able to change the name and description by changing the text in the spaces provided. Click Save Template when you have finished editing the template.

Manage Categories

You are able to add or edit the categories in the system.

  1. Ensure you are signed in to your Easy Design administrator account. Check that ADMIN is displayed in the top menu bar.
  2. Click on Categories in the top menu bar, or navigate to /categories.
  3. To add a category enter its name in the New Category panel. Click Add when you're done.
  4. To edit a category, click the pencil icon next to its name in the Existing Categories panel. Enter a new name for the category and click the save icon.
