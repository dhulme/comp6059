Introduction

EasyDesign is a new startup company launching a service to enable customers to quickly produce well designed logos, posters and flyers based on user generated content.

Users upload their created design templates to the site. They can then add placeholders over the top of their design where other users can add customised text. For example, an event poster template may have placeholders for the name, description, location and contact details.
