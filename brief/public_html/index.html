Introduction

EasyDesign is a new startup company launching a service to enable customers such as charities and organisations to quickly produce well designed logos, posters and flyers based on user generated content.

The site will make money by taking a cut from sales of paid content, and through advertising.

User stories

1. Upload content

Content creators upload their created design templates to the site. They can then use the online editor to add placeholders over the top of their design where other users can add customised text. For example, an event poster template may have placeholders for the name, description, location and contact details.

If a creator's content receives good reviews and a set number of downloads, they become legible to upload paid content.

2. Browsing content

Customers can search and browse the site to discover the content they are looking for. They can search by content type (e.g. logos, posters, flyers), by creator name or by search term.

They can also visit the "What's hot" page to view designs with a high number of downloads and ratings.

3. Downloading content

When a customer finds a design they like, they can add content to the design placeholders and download a high quality version of the design to print/upload to their website.

4. Reviewing content

When a customer has downloaded a design, they gain permission to rate and comment on it. The system can then recommend additional content to customer based upon this information.
